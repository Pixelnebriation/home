About

Pixelnebriation is a meet-up event for people starting out, or wanting to get into the gaming industry, as well as people who are already well established. People who are also just interested in gaming are also welcome! It provides a 'safe space' for people who may not be comfortable with socialising or networking, to talk with others similar folks. Leading to possible collaberations, or new friendships. It also provides the chance to talk to experienced people in the industry.

The locations of each Pixelnebriation event vary around London, but are always in casual and fun places such as Loading Bar in Dalston. The next event will be located at The Association for UK Interactive Entertainment (UKIE) building in Hatton Wall.
